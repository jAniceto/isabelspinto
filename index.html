About me

I graduated in Chemical Engineering, in 2010, at Faculty of Engineering, University of Porto (FEUP) and completed my PhD in Environmental Engineering in the same institution in 2015.

For 7 years (3 in pre-graduation), my research work in Associated Laboratory REQUIMTE-LAQV, at FEUP, was focused on biodegradable chelating agents (complexation and applications) and hydrometallurgical methods to recover metals from spent catalysts, with reduced environmental impact. Head on to the Publications section for information on published papers and participation in scientific conferences.

Currently I am working as a researcher at RAIZ institute, a forestry and paper research center of The Navigator Company.

Thanks for visiting my website.
